<h4>Outdoor Adventure Learning Centre (OALC) 5 Days 4 Nights</h4>
<p></p>
<p>Objectives</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Develop core skills and competencies that will enable them to enhance
physical health and well-being, identify hazards and manage risks to protect
self and others during outdoor activities.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Bond strongly as a class and as a cohort through team-based activities,
valuing diversity in the group dynamics and contributing effectively to
group goals.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Acquire social awareness and apply interpersonal skills to build and maintain
positive relationships based on mutual respect.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Develop confidence, resilience and leadership through team-based learning
and collaboration.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Develop an appreciation for the outdoors and demonstrate care for the
environment.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>Proposed programme should incorporate the learning of:
<br>1. School Values (i-CREST)
<br><em>(Integrity, Care, Resilience, Excellence, Self-Worth, and Teamwork)</em>
</p>
<p></p>
<p>2. Social-Emotional Learning (SEL) Competencies
<br><em>(Self-Awareness, Self-Management, Social Awareness, Relationship Management &amp; Responsible Decision-Making)</em>
</p>
<p></p>
<h4>IgnITE Skills Challenge</h4>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">

</div>
<p></p>
<p>Objectives</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Introduces students to technical skills and fosters a deeper understanding
of courses offered by ITE</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Challenges are designed to be engaging and fun, to make learning enjoyable
and memorable</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>All participants will be required to attend one training workshop by ITE
or take up the Applied Learning Module (ApLM@ITE) linked to the Game Challenge.
This training will equip them with the necessary skills for the Challenge.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p></p>
<p>ITE will issue everyone with e-certificates of participation. On top of
that, the top 10 teams of each Challenge will be shortlisted to compete
in the Finals, where they could receive medals and trophies. Click <a href="https://www.ite.edu.sg/ignite-skills-challenge" rel="noopener nofollow" target="_blank">here</a> to
find out more.</p>
