<p>The President's Award for Teachers (PAT) gives national recognition to
the critical role that teachers play in moulding the future of our nation.
It pays tribute to excellent teachers who, through words and deeds, inspire
both their students and peers.&nbsp;
<br>
</p>
<p>PAT is open to teachers <strong>presently in service at a primary school, secondary school, junior college/Millennia Institute, Polytechnic, Institute of Technical Education, or National Institute of Early Childhood Development</strong>.&nbsp;</p>
