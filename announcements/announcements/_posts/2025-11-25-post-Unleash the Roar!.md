<p>Crest Secondary School is proud to be part of the <a href="https://www.unleashtheroar.com.sg/post/list-of-school-football-academy" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">School Football Academy</a> (SFA)
programme under the Unleash The Roar! national football project.</p>
<p></p>
<p><a href="http://www.unleashtheroar.com.sg/" rel="noopener noreferrer nofollow" target="_blank">Unleash The Roar!</a> was
launched in 2021 as a national initiative to uplift Singapore's football
and develop the next generation of football talent.</p>
<p></p>
<p>Through the SFA programme, Crest Secondary School student-athletes will
receive a holistic secondary school education where emphasis is placed
on values and characteristics that help students develop both on the field
and in the classrooms. The SFA programme prides itself on having a team
of quality coaches, an optimised daily training environment and sport science
expertise.</p>
